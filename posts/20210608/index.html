TTT Reborn

Matthew Zegar, Jun 9, 2021

I’m currently taking a break from my original “The Specialists” project to work on TTT Reborn. As someone who has played thousands of hours of the original TTT, I am excited to be a part of the team that is remaking the gamemode for s&box.

Networking in s&box

I haven’t had the opportunity yet to take any networking classes at my university yet. It made me a bit worried when I decided to write some networking code needed for TTT Reborn. However, it was a breeze. It’s literally as simple as just adding a decorator around a variable to network it to other players. This ease of use allowed me to implement a fully fledged round system, and a body inspecting system.

Here is a quick snippet of code…

[Net, Local] // Now networked locally to players!
public RoleType Role { get; set; }
